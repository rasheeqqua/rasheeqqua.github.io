Prospectus of MNEC

Goal

To organize and perform events and interactive study tours for spreading the knowledge of Nuclear Science & Engineering and creating awareness to promote the safe use of Nuclear Energy. To organize and perform events and interactive study tours for spreading the knowledge of Nuclear Science & Engineering and creating awareness to promote the safe use of Nuclear Energy. To encourage the school & college level students of Bangladesh in the application of Nuclear Energy as well as creating a brand value of MIST in their mind.


Motivation

The world has already advanced in the field of nuclear science. World leading engineering universities (such as MIT, University of Toronto etc.) have already spent more than 60 years in academic and research activities of nuclear science. Nuclear Science and Engineering Department of MIST, established in 2015, has only started their journey. Hence, only academic activities wonâ€™t be enough to keep pace with the rest of the world. A central hub has to be established to encourage students in research works through sharing technical knowledge and experiences with professionals who have spent a significant portion of their life for the advancement of this field.


Strategy

Advancement in nuclear technology can only be achieved through the combined efforts of students from varied disciplines. Besides, the general people have to be assured that the beneficial aspects of nuclear energy outperform its negative impacts by a large margin. To ensure excellence of knowledge in nuclear field and to create a general awareness in public, the current situation demands the need for a nuclear engineering club.

Executive Committee

Panel Members

A MNEC Executive Committee shall be formed comprising of a President, two Vice Presidents, one General Secretary, a single Team Director for each of the Communication, Publication and Event Organizing team, Treasurer and nine (9) Executive Members evenly distributed in each of the team.

The tenure of service of a MNEC Executive Committee shall be no more than two MIST terms after which a new committee must be proposed by the present committee. Proposition for new committee must be made with neutral judgment of past works of MNEC Members. The Executive Committee under decision of major committee members shall hold the power to register members and patrons of MNEC.

If any allegation of misconduct, uncooperative, and hostile behavior is reported against an executive member of the committee, and if he/she is found guilty after proper investigation, then the accused member will be immediately removed from his/her respective position in the committee.

Activities

Academic Activites

We have got alumni from world renowned universities such as: MEPhI, Tsinghua University, Purdue University etc. Besides, most of our alumni are associated with academic professions and research activites. Current students focus on having a sound knowledge about nuclear energy and technologies.

Technical Assistance

The students of MIST Nuclear Engineering Club are extremely adept in Neutronics Calculations and Monte Carlo Simulation toolkits such as GEANT4, OPENMC etc. The club is planning to assist the students in becoming more acquainted with Python programming language and MATLAB software.

Prefessional Career

Members of MNEC have been appointed in government institutions such as BAERA, Bangladesh Army etc. There are many potential job sectors availble for the members besides the above mentioned two. RNPP, BAEC, NPCBL are examples of such institutions.
